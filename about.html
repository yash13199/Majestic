Welcome To Hotel Majestic

LIVE A UNIQUE AND UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE

Hotel Majestic is an elegant turn of the century boutique hotel located in the Victorian neighborhood of Pacific Heights, San Francisco. Hotel Majestic proudly holds the title of the oldest and longest operating hotel in San Francisco. Built in 1902, the well preserved historic building has architecture from the early 20th century, in addition to the French and English antique ornaments with all the comforts of the modern age.

Hotel Majestic has an appreciable variety of well appointed rooms for our guests to choose from. Guest rooms are decorated in our signature Victorian style and equipped with all the essentials to have a pleasant stay in our hotel. Our facilities include complimentary continental breakfast served in our Cafe Majestic dining room.

Make use of our recently updated gym open daily from (AM-PM). Unwind at the Butterfly lounge located off of the lobby, where you can purchase cocktails and bar bites from (hours of operation). Our friendly front desk staff is ready and excited to offer concierge services and universal guidance on nearby attractions and transportation services. We encourage you to discover the history of Hotel Majestic and relish in all our hotel has to offer in the heart of San Francisco.
